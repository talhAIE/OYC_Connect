Depending on your app's features, you may safely ignore these sections for OYC Connect.

### App Clip
*(Optional)*
An App Clip is a small part of your app that’s discoverable when it’s needed and lets people complete a task quickly without downloading the full app (e.g., using an NFC tag to immediately load a Stripe donation page).
*   **Do you need this?** **No.** Unless you specifically programmed an App Clip target in Xcode for the OYC Connect app, you can completely ignore this section. Leave it blank.

### iMessage App
*(Optional)*
This section is for apps that include sticker packs or iMessage extensions (like custom keyboards or apps that run directly inside the iOS Messages app).
*   **Do you need this?** **No.** OYC Connect does not use the Messages framework. Leave this section entirely blank.
